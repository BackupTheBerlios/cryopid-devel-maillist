Michele Alberti schrieb:
> Debian is supported, even better than Ubuntu.
> The problem is the stable version that is shipped with memory
> randomization. And memory randomization is the reason why you get segfault
> on it.
> I'm looking for a method to disable it.

If you add the lines

vm.mmap_min_addr = 4096
vm.vdso_enabled = 2

to your /etc/sysctl.conf and reload it with "sysctl -p", it works, at
least with Ubuntu 8.04. (nothing else testet yet)
