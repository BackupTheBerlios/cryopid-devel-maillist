I have added a white board to the CryoPID wiki:

http://sharesource.org/project/cryopid/wiki/Whiteboard/#

This is a good place to collaborate on why certain things should exist
and how they should be implemented. This lets newcomers to the project
see our discussions without having to wade through / read the mailing
list archives.

The first entry is related to test coverage analysis, as discussed this
evening on IRC. Feel free to add additional nodes.

Also, just pointing out, we have a bug tracking system here:

http://sharesource.org/project/cryopid/bugs/

Please encourage people reporting bugs on the list or via IRC to enter a
bug report. I _think_ we can pipe that to this list, I'm pretty sure SS2
supports it, I just don't see a way of doing it. I'll inquire regarding
that.
