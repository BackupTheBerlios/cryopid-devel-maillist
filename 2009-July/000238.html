I once hit a similar issue with PID cacheing, which seemed to appear
in glibc a few years ago. In my application, I found that I could get
the new PID for the restarted process by just making a syscall to
read the environment variable $PPID. One other suggestion from someone
(which I haven't tried) was to look at /proc/self/stat, which should
include the PID somewhere.
