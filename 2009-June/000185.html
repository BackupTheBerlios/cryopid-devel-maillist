On Thu, 2009-06-18 at 00:10 -0700, Valery Reznic wrote:

> Better change CryoPID to mmap to address >= 65536

Not so easy, we would have to save the lowest address as part of the
frozen PID, so that when un-freezing, it could tell the user why it
won't work. I.e. on system A (where a pid was frozen) its 65536, on B
its 0, or vice versa.

A great many of cryopid users work with much older kernels and distros.

I am not opposed to making the starting address floating, but sanity
when un-freezing is important .. it should 'just work', otherwise we're
back in the same place of Debian dealing with our quirks.
