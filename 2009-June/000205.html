> > custom configuration such as the start value.

> OK, at least now it's work somehow :)
> Why .cryopidrc and not additional flag ?

This seemed the easiest way, especially when considering distribution
packagers.

Everything in the .rc file can be specified on the command line when
invoking freeze, command line options will of course override whatever
is read in the .rc file.

It works out to be a much better experience for users who need a degree
of portability from machine to machine without having to deal with
complex command line options (unless they want to).

Setting it at compile time didn't seem proper either.
