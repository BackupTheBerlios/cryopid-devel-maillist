On Wed, 2009-06-17 at 23:07 -0700, Valery Reznic wrote:

> It's looks like mmap failed because /proc/sys/vm/mmap_min_addr is set. If you
> reset it to 0 your program should work (or at least fail later in the other
> place :)
> 
> Valery

Its probably also worth mentioning that many distros now set this
(somewhere) in /etc/sysctl.d .. if your going to be working with
CryoPID, its probably a good idea to comment that out.

On Ubuntu, its in 10-process-security.conf , which now defaults 65536.

This brings to light that we don't handle mmap() failing in many places,
could just be a matter of wrapping it in cryopid_mmap() with an
assertion?

It may also be worth adding a cryopid_sanity() in addition to the above,
which actually tells users why things won't work. That could go for
system settings, PAX, etc.

The idea is, we reduce the number of cryptic bugs that Debian
maintainers have to deal with. If they build with NDEBUG and package it,
well, they must be more confident than we are :)
